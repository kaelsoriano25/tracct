Traact Content Strategy

The LinkedIn Content Strategy

Positioning Traact as the Modern Legal CRM

This presentation outlines a detailed content strategy for both Adam Friedman's personal profile and the Traact company page, designed to establish market authority and drive qualified leads.

The Core Strategy & Pillars

Core Messaging

"Your Legal CRM for Entity Management." We will pivot from a generic "all-in-one" solution to a specific identity that resonates with legal and finance professionals.

1. Efficiency Pillar

Address cost-savings and budget optimization. Attract senior leaders with topics on reducing spend and streamlining workflows.

2. Product Pillar

Visually showcase the modern design and core functionalities of the Traact platform through demos and "before & after" content.

3. Human Pillar

Build trust and community by sharing client testimonials, team insights, and engaging directly with the audience.

Content Templates

Case Study Post

Tell a concise story with a clear problem and quantified solution.

  • Start with a bold stat (e.g., "Saved 200+ Hours").
  • Describe the manual process and pain points.
  • List 3 clear benefits from using Traact.
  • Link to the full case study on the website.

Client Testimonial Post

Leverage social proof with a direct, personal quote.

  • Use a professional headshot or company logo.
  • Begin with a rewarding statement.
  • Feature a direct quote from the client.
  • Link to the team or about page to humanize the brand.

90-Day Action Plan

Month 1: Foundation & Messaging

Establish the "Legal CRM" messaging on both profiles. Post initial infographics and a testimonial. Begin active engagement with the target audience.

Month 2: Content & Curation

Increase posting frequency and create a content library. Publish new infographics, short videos, and a full client interview.

Month 3: Amplification & Refinement

Analyze what content is performing best and amplify it. Use data to refine the strategy and promote top posts with advertising.
